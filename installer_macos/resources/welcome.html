Welcome to the Imaginify Installer

Imaginify is a modern desktop image tool for resizing, cropping, compressing and converting images — in single or batch mode.

This installer will guide you through the steps to install Imaginify on your Mac:

  • Imaginify.app will be installed into /Applications.
  • You can launch it afterwards from Launchpad or Spotlight.
  • To uninstall later, drag Imaginify.app to the Trash.
